HealthyScan Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

HealthyScan ("the App", "we", "us") is a food label scanning app that helps users in the UK understand the ingredients and nutrition information of food products. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and what choices you have.

Information We Collect

HealthyScan is designed to keep your data on your device wherever possible.

  • Scan data: When you scan a food label, take a photo, or enter ingredients/nutrition information manually, this data is processed in order to provide you with a health analysis. Scan history is stored locally on your device using on-device storage and is not uploaded to any server controlled by us.
  • Profile and preferences: Information you enter in the Customise section (such as dietary preferences, health goals, and allergies or intolerances you specify) is stored locally on your device and used only to personalise results shown to you within the App.
  • Shopping list: Items you add to your shopping list are stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Processing — AI Analysis

To analyse ingredients, nutrition information, and food label images, and to generate health scores, summaries, and recipe suggestions, the App sends relevant scan data (such as photographed labels, typed ingredient lists, or nutrition values) to a third-party AI provider (Groq) for processing. This data is sent via a secure proxy operated by us and is used only to generate the analysis shown to you. It is not used by us for any other purpose.

Please avoid including personally identifiable information in any text or photo you submit for analysis, as this data is processed by the third-party AI provider in order to generate your results.

Advertising

HealthyScan displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data such as your device's advertising identifier to show ads, including personalised ads based on your interests. This may involve the use of cookies or similar technologies and the collection of device information.

You can manage your ad personalisation settings, including opting out of personalised advertising, through your device's settings (Settings > Privacy > Ads on most Android devices) or via Google's Ads Settings at adssettings.google.com.

For more information on how Google uses data through AdMob, please see Google's Partner Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Data Storage and Retention

Scan history, preferences, and shopping list data are stored locally on your device. You can clear your scan history, shopping list, or reset your preferences at any time from within the App. Clearing this data removes it from your device. We do not retain copies of this data on any server.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with the third parties described above (the AI analysis provider, via our secure proxy, and Google AdMob for advertising) as necessary to provide the App's features.

Children's Privacy

HealthyScan does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App does not require account creation, and all user data described above is stored locally on the user's device. If you believe a child has provided us with information through the App in a way that concerns you, please contact us using the details below.

Your Rights

If you are located in the UK or EU, you have rights under UK GDPR / GDPR regarding your personal data, including the right to access, delete, or correct your data. Since most data is stored locally on your device, you can exercise these rights directly by clearing data within the App or uninstalling the App. For questions about data processed by third-party services (AI analysis or advertising), please refer to the relevant third party's privacy policy linked above, or contact us below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
